# Revenue Model

## Target

The strategic target is an average of $200 per day, or roughly $6,000 per month. This is a business target, not a guaranteed output of the software.

## Revenue ladder

### 1. Free Opportunity Snapshot

Purpose: acquire qualified leads.

Automated delivery:

- public URL submission
- browser crawl
- mobile and desktop evidence
- opportunity score
- one evidence-backed finding
- private report link

### 2. Full Digital Audit

Default test price: **$49 one-time**.

Purpose: prove that the system can sell and deliver a digital product without a call.

Math to $6,000/month:

- 123 sales per month
- roughly 4 sales per day

This is traffic-intensive, so it should not carry the target alone.

### 3. Strategy Audit

Default test price: **$299 one-time**.

Purpose: add human review and turn automated evidence into a prioritized plan.

Math to $6,000/month:

- 21 sales per month
- roughly five sales per week

### 4. Implementation Sprint

Default anchor: **from $1,000**.

Purpose: monetize the highest-value opportunities discovered by the audit.

Math to $6,000/month:

- six $1,000 projects per month, or
- three $2,000 projects per month

This is the fastest early path to the target, but it still requires human delivery and sales judgment.

### 5. UberBond Watch

Default test price: **$99/month**.

Purpose: recurring revenue through scheduled re-audits and change monitoring.

Math to $6,000 MRR:

- 61 active subscriptions

A mixed portfolio is more realistic:

| Revenue source | Monthly example |
|---|---:|
| 2 implementation sprints at $1,500 | $3,000 |
| 5 strategy audits at $299 | $1,495 |
| 10 monitoring subscriptions at $99 | $990 |
| 11 full audits at $49 | $539 |
| **Total** | **$6,024** |

## Why this model was chosen

The research compared automated audits, micro-SaaS, newsletters, lead generation, digital products, and affiliate content. Automated audits ranked highest for the combination of:

- low startup cost
- short path to first revenue
- high service upsell value
- compatibility with design, web, branding, and scientific communication skills
- ability to evolve into a subscription product

## Metrics that matter

Track:

- website visitors
- audit-form completion rate
- report completion rate
- paid full-audit conversion
- strategy-audit conversion
- booked-call conversion
- implementation close rate
- monitoring subscription activation
- monthly recurring revenue
- refund rate
- cost per completed audit
- human hours per $1,000 revenue

Ignore vanity metrics unless they predict revenue.

## Validation gates

Do not scale to the next stage until the current one works.

1. **Research quality:** 20 test websites produce useful, supported findings.
2. **Lead interest:** at least three prospects engage with the report.
3. **Paid digital product:** at least one full or strategy audit is purchased.
4. **Implementation:** at least one audit becomes a paid project.
5. **Retention:** at least one customer accepts recurring monitoring.

## The honest automation goal

The system should automate discovery, analysis, delivery, payment matching, monitoring, and reporting. Mohamed should retain:

- market choice
- final creative judgment
- important sales conversations
- negotiation
- premium implementation
- exception handling
